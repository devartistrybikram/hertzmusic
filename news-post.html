    Hertz Guitars Unveils the Astra 6

    April 2
    2025

    Hertz Guitars has once again set a new benchmark in guitar innovation with the launch of the Astra 6 — a sleek, futuristic, and affordable headless guitar designed for musicians seeking ultimate playability and sonic versatility. Crafted with precision and cutting‑edge materials, the Astra 6 is as stylish as it is functional. It combines modern design with a powerful tonal foundation, making it a top choice for today’s forward‑thinking players.

    A New Era of Innovation in Headless Guitars

    The Astra 6’s construction starts with an ash body topped with maple, paired with a bolt‑on, 3‑piece roasted maple neck and maple fretboard featuring 24 frets. Its scale length is 26.5", offering players tighter low end and clarity. The electronics include Wilkinson humbuckers, and a 3‑way pickup selector, with a push/pull coil‑tap on the tone knob for switching between humbucking and single‑coil tones. Hardware and bridge/tuners follow the headless design, and the finishes are available in Natural, Transparent Black, and Transparent Red with a high‑gloss coating.

    Key Specifications

    The headless configuration of the Astra 6 isn’t just about aesthetics — it delivers functional benefits. Players get reduced weight, improved balance, and better tuning stability, especially during longer sessions. The Wilkinson humbuckers provide rich, powerful tones that work well for rock, metal, jazz, fusion, and more. The coil‑tap functionality expands tonal flexibility, giving access to hum‑bucker power and crisp single‑coil clarity with a simple push/pull action.

    Performance Meets Precision

    Astra 6 is offered in three appealing high‑gloss finishes: Natural, Transparent Black, and Transparent Red. These finishes highlight the wood grain and contribute to the guitar’s aggressive yet elegant visual character, matching its sonic performance with visual impact.

    Striking Finishes

    According to Ronny Sinha from Hertz Guitars’ Product Development team, “The Astra 6 is a game‑changer for modern guitarists. It combines top‑tier craftsmanship with cutting‑edge design, ensuring players get the best in tone, feel, and aesthetics.” This endorsement reflects the company’s confidence in both the build quality and performance of the instrument.

    What the Experts Say

    The Astra 6 comes loaded with features that cater to both style and versatility: the headless design for comfort and balance; premium tonewoods (ash and maple) for rich resonance; a roasted 3‑piece maple neck for stability; extended scale length for tight low‑end; versatile electronics with coil‑tap; and a finish lineup that both protects and showcases the wood. These all combine to make it a serious option for players wanting both form and function.

    Key Features

    Whether you’re performing on stage or recording in the studio, the Astra 6 delivers everything a modern guitarist could ask for — playability, tone, innovation, and style, all at an accessible price point. It marks a step forward for headless guitars in India, especially for musicians who value both aesthetics and performance. Experience the future of guitar playing. Experience the Astra 6.
